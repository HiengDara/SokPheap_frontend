SokPheap is a health monitoring app designed to help you manage and prevent noncommunicable diseases (NCDs) like diabetes and hypertension. It uses AI to analyze your health data—collected through wearable devices or manual input—and provides personalized recommendations to improve your health.

SokPheap offers three main features: Symptom Detection (identifies early signs of health issues), Health Advisor (provides personalized advice), and LifeSync (offers diet and fitness plans). These features work together to help you stay on top of your health.
